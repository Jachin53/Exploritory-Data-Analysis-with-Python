# 📊 Exploratory Data Analysis with Python – Resale Shop Dataset

## 📌 Overview
This project demonstrates **exploratory data analysis (EDA)** using Python on a resale shop dataset.  
The analysis focuses on uncovering **business insights** such as customer segmentation, spending behavior, and revenue drivers.

Key steps include:
- Data ingestion and cleaning
- Statistical summaries
- Feature engineering (date extraction, age classification, spending buckets)
- Revenue analysis by demographic and product categories
- Visualizations for clear business insights

---

## 📂 Libraries Used
- **pandas** – data manipulation and aggregation  
- **numpy** – numerical operations  
- **matplotlib** – data visualization  

---

## 📈 Visualizations Implemented

### 1. Revenue by Age Classification
- Grouped by `Age_Classification`
- Bar chart showing total revenue contribution per age group
- Labels added directly on bars for clarity

### 2. Revenue by Spending Bucket
- Grouped by `Spending_Bucket`
- Bar chart highlighting customer spending behavior
- Useful for identifying high-value vs low-value customers

### 3. Revenue by Product Category
- Grouped by `Product Category`
- Bar chart comparing revenue across product categories
- Helps identify top-performing items

### 4. Revenue by Gender
- Grouped by `Gender`
- Pie chart showing proportional revenue contribution
- Includes percentage labels and legend with actual revenue values

### 5. Revenue per Day
- Grouped by `Day`
- Bar chart showing daily revenue trends
- Useful for identifying peak sales days

### 6. Revenue per Month
- Grouped by `Month`
- Bar chart showing monthly revenue distribution
- Highlights seasonality and growth patterns

---

## 🎯 Business Insights
- **Customer Segmentation:** Age, gender, and spending buckets reveal who drives revenue.
- **Product Performance:** Product category analysis highlights profitable items.
- **Temporal Trends:** Daily and monthly breakdowns uncover peak sales periods and seasonality.
- **Strategic Value:** These insights can guide marketing campaigns, inventory planning, and customer engagement strategies.
